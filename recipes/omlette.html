Yummy Omlette

Description

This recipe makes a yummy omlette

Ingredients

  • Salted Butter

  • Eggs

  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions

  1. Prepare the Filling

  2. Begin by whisking the eggs vigorously until they are homogenous

  3. Next, melt the butter in an 8‚Äù nonstick skillet over medium-low heat.

  4. Then, add the whisked eggs and swirl them in the pan until they are evenly distributed.

  5. Next, cover the pan and cook over medium-low heat until the eggs begin to set.

  6. Once they begin to set, use a spatula to gently push the edges in slightly and swirl the pan so that any uncooked eggs on the top pour over the sides to the edges of the pan and cook.

  7. Once the omelette is mostly set (about 5-6 minutes), carefully flip it and turn off the heat.

  8. Sprinkle the cheese over the top of the omelette and add the toppings on one half of the omelette.

  9. Then, gently fold the other half over the toppings.

  10. Emjoy!
